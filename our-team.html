SUCCESS TEAM

Success Team is a group of students from Accra Technical University with similar goals, who have partnered together to establish this plateform to enlighten and broaden the scope of knowledge of other students who finds it difficult to grasp the concepts of sofwares such as Autocad, Solid Works, Programming etc and as well be equipped with the latest softwares mentioned above. We have been in existence for over 3 years,stretching hands to deliver genuine help to students from all around the world and even to institutions who might find it necessary to brigthen their employers' knowledge in the softwares.


Mr Fancis Sagoe,a Production Engineering Student from Accra Technical Student is one of the founding member of the Sucess Team. He is also a Front-End Web developer to this plateform and other websites. He is a tutor in the AutoCAD tutorials and has tremendous teaching skills and poised to imbibe the necesary knowledge you woukd need in this programs
